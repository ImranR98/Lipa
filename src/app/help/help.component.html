Help

Lipa calculates the amount each person owes when splitting a reciept.

The Main page consists of 2 tabs - 'Items' and 'People'. The first tab asks for the list of items on the reciept, and the second tab asks for the list of people, and the item distribution.

Items tab

  1. Fill in the Item name, cost, quantity, and taxable status of the first item on your reciept.
  2. Click '+ Add Item' to add the second item, and repeat as above, until all items are listed.
  3. The trashcan icon is used to delete the item.

People tab

  1. Fill in the first person's name.
  2. Check the boxes for each item the person was involved in buying.
  3. Click '+ Add Person' to add the second person, and repeat as above, until all people are listed.
  4. The trashcan icon is used to delete the person.
